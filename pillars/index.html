Meridian/Pillars

The Sixteen Pillars

Meridian is built on sixteen interlocking pillars organized into three groups: the core product, the platform infrastructure, and the content engine.

Direct Answer Meridian consists of sixteen pillars. Pillars 1–8 are the core product — the Constitution, Trust Score, Threat Feed, Incident Docket, Weekly Ruling, Arena, Directory, and Scanner. Pillars 9–10 are infrastructure — the Machine Interface Layer and Discovery Pipeline. Pillars 11–16 are the content engine — editorial series, programmatic content, SEO, AEO, agent-readable content, and editorial operations. Each pillar generates recurring engagement and feeds data into the others.
