Standards

The canonical behavioral rules of the agentic economy — versioned, governed, and machine-readable.

Direct Answer Meridian publishes two families of standards. The Constitution (UAOP) defines the seven universal principles that apply to every agent regardless of deployment context. The Domain Conduct Codes add obligations that apply in specific verticals — finance, medicine, law, security research, customer service, and government. All standards are versioned using semantic versioning, governed by the Meridian Board, and published in structured JSON at /v1/standards.
